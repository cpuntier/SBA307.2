RYU

Ever training, this martial artist seeks true strength. Well-mannered and sincere, Ryu travels the globe in search of worthy opponents. Having overcome the Satsui no Hado, he now seeks yet greater heights. Ryu embodies all the meaningful aspects of Street Fighter gameplay in one concise package. Ryu emphasizes a patient playstyle with strong defensive tools.

Character Type

Type:Standard

Effective Range: Mid-Range

Ease of Use: Normal

Name Input Description

Hadoken		 QCF + P Fire a ki blast directly forward. Useful for attacking opponents at range.

Shoryuken		 DP Leap into the air and perform a powerful uppercut. A Shoryuken is invincible to an opponent's jumping attacks, making it a great anti-air tool.

Tatsumaki Senpu-kyaku		 QCB+K Unleash a series of roundhouse kicks while moving forward. A useful attack for knocking opponents away.

Aerial Tatsumaki Senpu-kyaku		 QCB+K (in air) Unleash a series of roundhouse kicks while moving forward. A useful attack for knocking opponents away.

High Blade Kick		 QCF+K A thrust kick that quickly closes distance, and can be used to surprise an opponent between their attacks. Strike with the tip of the foot for maximum effectiveness.

Hashogeki		 QCB+P Concentrate ki in the palm and unleash it directly forward. Useful as a follow-up after blocks and combos.

Denjin Charge 		DD+P Envelop your fists with the Power of Nothingness, enhancing moves such as the Hadoken and Hashogeki. Leaves you wide open, making the timing of its usage critical.
