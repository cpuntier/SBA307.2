Chun Li

A former high-kicking ICPO agent, Chun-Li looks after Li-Fen, a victim of the Black Moon Incident. With Shadaloo sundered, she now runs kung fu classes, and has become a well-loved member of the local community. Chun-Li is a tried and true footsies character, with great emphasis on having many options to control space and punish her opponents in neutral. She has a little bit of everything when it comes to her general tools, but for the most part they're tailored toward whiff punishes and space control.

Character Type

Type:Speed

Effective Range: Mid-Range

Ease of Use: Hard

Name Input Description

Kikoken		 L R+P Fire a ki blast directly forward. Useful for attacking opponents at range.

Hundred Lightning Kicks		 DP Unleash a series of high-speed kicks. Useful for creating combos out of normal attacks.

Lightning Kick Barrage		 KK A series of kicks that can be performed following an OD Hundred Lightning Kicks. Useful for tacking on additional damage while also knocking down your opponent.

Aerial Hundred Lightning Kicks		 QCF+K (in air) A Hundred Lightning Kicks performed while jumping. Effective when used to convert a jumping attack into a combo.

Spinning Bird Kick		 QCF+K A series of rotating, upside-down kicks with your legs extended wide. This move does lots of damage, and is useful when performing combos against opponents that have left themselves wide open.

Hazanshu		 QCB+K Leap forward, rotate in the air, and slam your leg downward with a powerful kick. Useful for checking an opponent's sweeps, or as a way to close distance.

Tensho Kicks 		DD+K Rise into the air with multiple kicks. Useful for countering an opponent's jumping attacks, or as a way to close out combos.
